Shipping & Delivery Information

  1. Home/
  2. Shipping & Delivery Information

At OnlineSolutionHub X Shiv Shakti Enterprises we understand how important it is for you to receive your orders on time and in perfect condition. That’s why we’ve partnered with the best carriers to ensure a seamless shipping and delivery experience. Please take a moment to read through our shipping policies to help answer any questions you may have.

 

Order Processing Time
Standard Orders: All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed after 3:00 PM will be processed the next business day.
Pre-Orders & Backordered Items: If your order contains a pre-order or backordered item, it will ship once the full order is in stock.
Order Confirmation: After placing your order, you will receive a confirmation email with your order details. Once your order ships, we’ll send you a tracking number so you can follow your package.

 

Shipping Methods & Delivery Times
We offer a variety of shipping methods to fit your needs. Delivery times are calculated based on the shipping method selected and the destination.
Standard Shipping: Delivery Time: 3-7 business days (within the continental india) Cost: Free for orders over INR 500; otherwise, a flat rate of INR.
Expedited Shipping: Delivery Time: 2-3 business days Cost: 100rs
Express Shipping: Delivery Time: 1 business day Cost: 200 (for select locations only)

 

Shipping Fees & Promotions .
Free Shipping: We offer free standard shipping on orders over Rs 5000 within the India. (before taxes and shipping costs).
Promo Codes: If you have a discount or promo code, you can apply it at checkout to save on shipping costs.

 

Track Your Order
ce your order has shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information. You can track the status of your package through our carrier’s website or by visiting the Track Your Order section on our site. If you have any issues with tracking, feel free to reach out to us.
